Grace Hopper

U.S. Navy
- recalled for active duty twice, once in 1966 and then again in 1971
- received highest non-combat award
- was in a all female division called (WAVES)
- In 1959 she was in a association called "Conference on Data Systems Languages" (CODASYL)
- This is where she helped developed the (COBOL) which is a programming language and other software
- COBOL stands for "Common Business Oriented Language", it is still in use today to process business software today
